# crossvalid

Cross-validated analysis. Not information summaries.

The name is the method: check claims from multiple independent angles, confirm what holds up, discard what doesn't. Most wrong information doesn't look wrong — it looks cited, credentialed, and peer-reviewed. We exist to catch what slips through.
